Chicken and Waffles

Description

This recipe has crunch, sweet and spicy, savory all in one.

  • Prep time: 15 mins
  • Cook time: 30 mins
  • Additional time: 20 mins
  • Total time: 1 hr 5 mins
  • Yield: 4 sandwiches

Ingredients

  1. 4 eggs
  2. 1/4 cup heavy cream
  3. 2 tablespoons cayenne pepper
  4. 1 tablespoon salt
  5. 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
  6. 2 cups all-purpose flour
  7. 1 cup cornstarch
  8. 1 tablespoon salt
  9. 1 quart peanut oil for frying
  10. 8 chicken tenders
  11. 1 cup mayonnaise
  12. 1/4 cup maple syrup
  13. 2 teaspoons prepared horseradish
  14. 1 teaspoon dry mustard powder
  15. 12 slices bacon
  16. 8 thin slices Cheddar cheese
  17. 8 plain frozen waffles
